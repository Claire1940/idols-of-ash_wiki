## Mastering the Abseiling Mechanics

The core of *Idols of Ash* lies in its verticality. Unlike traditional horror games where you run through hallways, here you are constantly suspended over a lethal drop. In the **idols of ash new game plus** environment, precision becomes even more critical as the margin for error narrows.

| Action | Control (Default) | Expert Tip for NG+ |
|---|---|---|
| **Throw Grapple** | Right Click | Aim for wooden beams or rough stone edges for the most secure connection. |
| **Slide Down Rope** | Left Ctrl | Use short bursts of sliding to maintain control and avoid gaining too much momentum. |
| **Climb Up Rope** | Space | Essential for correcting over-swings or reaching higher platforms after a fall. |
| **Swing Momentum** | A / D Keys | Build a wide arc before releasing to clear large gaps between temple platforms. |

> **💡 Tip:** Your rope has a specific length displayed in the top-left of the UI. Always check this before committing to a long drop, as running out of line mid-descent often leads to a fatal plunge.

## Survival Tactics: The Great Centipede

The primary antagonist of the game is a massive, multi-legged horror that tracks your vibrations and movement. In a standard playthrough, the creature is terrifying; however, in **idols of ash new game plus**, its AI becomes significantly more aggressive. It will take more direct paths to intercept you and give you less "breathing room" between sections.

To survive the hunt, you must balance speed with stealth. The centipede reacts to the sound of your grapple hooking into surfaces and the friction of your rope.

1.  **Listen for the Chittering:** The creature makes distinct clicking sounds when it is nearby. If the sound grows rapid, it has spotted you.
2.  **Use Verticality as a Shield:** The centipede is fast on flat surfaces but struggles with complex vertical transitions. Use your rope to skip large sections of stairs.
3.  **Vitality Management:** Collecting latent embers is your only way to heal. In NG+, these are often placed in riskier locations.

## The Role of Ancient Ashes and Lore

Throughout the descent, you will encounter urns containing ancient ashes. Breathing these in triggers "illusions" or memories of the protagonist's past—specifically regarding a village and a mysterious woman. In **idols of ash new game plus**, these sequences serve as more than just narrative beats; they provide brief moments of respite where the centipede cannot reach you.

> **⚠️ Warning:** While the ash visions provide safety, they also coat your lungs and throat, momentarily blurring your vision when you return to the "real" world. Use this time to plan your next three grapple points.

The narrative suggests that those who enter the abyss meet their regrets. By completing the game multiple times, players can piece together the true nature of the "coil rot" and the sickness that claimed the protagonist's village. There are community theories suggesting that finding every ash urn in a single NG+ run may unlock a secret "Absolution" ending, though this remains a challenge for the most dedicated explorers.

## Strategy for the Final Descent

The final sequence of the game requires you to drop through a series of broken platforms while the centipede is in a state of constant pursuit. In **idols of ash new game plus**, you cannot afford to stop and think. You must "pre-aim" your grapple while still in free-fall.

1.  **Look Down:** Always peer into the distance (Right Click) to identify the next green glow of a vitality ember or the grey of a stone platform.
2.  **Swing High:** Don't just drop straight down. Swinging horizontally can help you reach "safe zones" that the centipede cannot easily crawl to.
3.  **Don't Panic:** The music will swell and the chittering will get louder. Focus on the reticle and the length of your rope.

## FAQ

**Q: Does Idols of Ash have an official New Game Plus button?**

A: While the game doesn't always feature a traditional "NG+" menu option in every version, the **idols of ash new game plus** experience is typically accessed by loading your completed save file, which restarts the journey with increased difficulty and persistent lore collectibles.

**Q: Can I kill the centipede in the game?**

A: No. *Idols of Ash* is a pure survival horror experience. There is no combat system. Your only defenses are your agility, your rope, and your ability to use the environment to outmaneuver the creature.

**Q: What happens if I fall into the bottomless pits?**

A: Falling into the darkness usually results in a game over, though the game is surprisingly forgiving with fall damage on solid ground. In New Game Plus, however, falling from any height greater than 10 meters will significantly drain your vitality or kill you instantly.

**Q: Who is the woman at the end of the game?**

A: The woman is a manifestation of the protagonist's memories. The "Face of Regret" lore suggests she is someone the player character left behind or failed to save during the sickness in their home village. The game implies that the journey into the ash is a metaphorical descent into grief.
