## Understanding the Shrine: The 10 Idols of Ash

Before attempting the dreams, you must visit the Shrine and activate all ten idols. This is the prerequisite for the **idols of ash xbox** achievements, specifically "Ashes in the Sky." Each idol provides a unique buff to your enemies, making the game significantly more punishing.

| Idol | Enemy Buff Provided | Strategy Note |
|---|---|---|
| **Acmon** | Enemies have extra armor | Use weapons with **Armor Ignore** perks. |
| **Garmuth** | Enemies deflect some damage | Avoid high-rate-of-fire weapons without lifesteal. |
| **Haph** | Enemies can retaliate on death | Keep your distance and use **Squirt Lures**. |
| **Lemael** | Enemies move and attack faster | Prioritize knockback to keep space. |
| **Mictia** | Enemies may turn invisible | Listen for audio cues and use homing attacks. |
| **Olmes** | Enemies drop bombs on death | Always stay mobile; never stand still. |
| **Pyth** | Enemies have increased health | Focus on **Critical Hit** synergies. |
| **Roia** | Enemies deal more damage | Essential to have **Lifeline** or **Hardy Punch**. |
| **Yeluth** | Enemies are harder to stagger | Use the **Brusher's Pike** for heavy hits. |
| **Zia** | Enemies regenerate health | Constant DPS is required to secure kills. |

To conquer the **idols of ash xbox** dreams, you need a balance of long-range crowd control and reliable melee defense. The combination of the **Brusher's Pike** and the **Calamity Cannon** is widely considered the gold standard for high-difficulty runs.

### Brusher's Pike
The Pike offers excellent reach and high single-target damage. Its most important perk for 10-idol runs is the ability to ignore armor, which counters the **Acmon** idol.

## Strategies for the Four Dreams

Each dream presents a different set of challenges. When playing for the **idols of ash xbox** achievements, you must adapt your positioning based on the specific arena layout.

### 1. The Smoking Pipe (The Kid's Dream)
The key strategy here is to remain on the starting platform. Use your Calamity Cannon to thin out the waves before they reach you. If enemies get too close, use the Pike's knockback to push them off the edges.
*   **Watch out for:** Spinning enemies that spawn toward the end. If they trap you on the platform, you will die instantly. Be prepared to roll into the main arena temporarily.
*   **Health Tip:** Save the crystal orbs that spawn at the end of waves. You can break them later for health regen if your tonics run low.

### 2. The Stock Pot (The Singer's Dream)
This dream features many armored and projectile-based enemies. Use the "Corner Strategy"—tuck yourself into a corner with a Skyway jump nearby for a quick escape.
*   **Queen Anne:** When facing the mini-bosses, dodge side-to-side rather than up and down. This makes their lunging attacks much easier to avoid.
*   **Birds:** Use **Squirt Lures** to distract the small birds, as they can quickly overwhelm your defenses.

### 3. The History Books (The Stranger's Dream)
This is widely considered the hardest part of the **idols of ash xbox** journey. The arena is full of hazards and high-damage foes like the Eiras.
*   **The Plank Strategy:** There is a long plank extending from the arena. Stay at the very tip. Most enemies will fail to path toward you and may even fall off the edge.
*   **Eira Priority:** If an Eira spawns, kill it immediately. Their homing attacks are one of the few things that can reliably hit you while you are on the plank.
*   **Hazard Warning:** Beware of the icicles near the plank; there is a collision bug that can cause you to fall through the map.

### 4. The Bedroll (The Survivor's Dream)
The final dream is actually more manageable than the Stranger's Dream if you use the environment correctly. Clear the debris on the far left side of the map and use the large spire as cover.
*   **Ledge Kills:** Many large enemies can be knocked off the ledge with the Pike. This is much faster than trying to chip away at their massive 10-idol health bars.
*   **Focus Fire:** Use your homing rockets to deal with distant turrets while you use the Pike to defend your immediate area.

## General Tips for Success

1.  **Don't Rush:** With the **Zia** idol active, enemies heal, but your primary concern should be avoiding damage. Take your time and pick off enemies one by one.
2.  **Shield Management:** Your shield is your best friend against the big birds in the Singer's Dream. Block to stun them, then counter with the Pike.
3.  **Squirt Lures:** Always have at least one Squirt Lure active during crowded waves. The AI will often prioritize the lure, giving you a few seconds of breathing room to reload or heal.
4.  **Armor Ignore:** Without the Armor Ignore perk on your Pike, the **Acmon** idol will make late-game enemies nearly invincible.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the most important spirit for the idols of ash xbox challenge?**

A: **Leachate** is arguably the most critical spirit. Because the idols make health tonics less efficient and enemies deal massive damage, the ability to regain health simply by attacking is the only way to stay at full health for the **Dead Rum** crit bonus.

**Q: Can I complete the 10-idol dreams in New Game Plus?**

A: Yes, and it is actually recommended. In New Game Plus, you will have access to all weapons and spirits from the start, making it much easier to optimize your build for the **idols of ash xbox** achievements.

**Q: Why are my rockets not seeking out enemies in the Stranger's Dream?**

A: The homing perk for the Calamity Cannon has a limited range. If you are using the "Plank Strategy," enemies off-screen or at the far end of the arena may not be targeted until they move closer to the edge of the screen.

**Q: Is there a specific level I should be for the Survivor's Dream?**

A: You should be Level 10. This ensures you have all 10 spirit slots available in the Distillery, which is necessary to stack enough defensive and offensive buffs to survive the 10-idol modifiers.
