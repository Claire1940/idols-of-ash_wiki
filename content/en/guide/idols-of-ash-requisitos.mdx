Navigating the scorched halls of Brume Tower requires more than just skill; you need to understand the **idols of ash requisitos** to fully conquer the Crown of the Old Iron King DLC. These mysterious statues, formally known as Ashen Idols, hold the fragmented soul of Nadalia, Bride of Ash. They act as both obstacles and lore-heavy objectives, often healing nearby enemies or inflicting curses on the player. To cleanse the tower and claim her true soul, players must meet specific **idols of ash requisitos**, including the collection of Smelter Wedges and key quest items from the base game. 

Whether you are seeking the powerful Outcry pyromancy or simply aiming for 100% completion in 2026, finding all 12 fragments is a monumental task. This guide breaks down every prerequisite, location, and boss encounter necessary to silence the Bride of Ash forever.

## Essential Items and Idols of Ash Requisitos

Before you can begin dismantling the Ashen Idols, you must secure several key items scattered throughout Brume Tower and the base game of Dark Souls 2. Without these, your progress will be halted by locked doors or inactive mechanisms.

| Item Name | Purpose | Location |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Smelter Wedges** | Used to destroy Ashen Idols | Found in stacks throughout the DLC |
| **Tower Key** | Opens various locked doors in Brume Tower | Found on a corpse in the lower levels |
| **Scorching Iron Scepter** | Activates the tower's elevators and machinery | Found in the foyer area |
| **Ashen Mist Heart** | Required to enter the Iron King's Memory | Obtained from the Ancient Dragon |
| **Hollow Skin / Cursebite Ring** | Mitigates the curse build-up from idols | Found behind an illusory wall in the DLC |

To fully satisfy the **idols of ash requisitos**, you must collect a total of 11 Smelter Wedges to destroy the 11 physical idols. The 12th fragment of Nadalia’s soul does not come from an idol but is dropped by the Fume Knight himself.

> **Warning:** Do not use all your Smelter Wedges on the first idols you see if you aren't prepared for the Fume Knight. Four idols surround his boss arena and can heal him during the fight if not destroyed.

## How to Obtain All 11 Smelter Wedges

The Smelter Wedges are the most critical component of the **idols of ash requisitos**. You cannot interact with an idol without having one in your inventory. These are distributed in three specific locations:

1.  **The Entryway (6 Wedges):** Upon first entering the Brume Tower area, before crossing the massive chain, check the corpse on the left. This provides your initial stack to start the quest.
2.  **The Barrel Hallway (4 Wedges):** Deeper in the tower, you will find a hallway filled with Iron Warriors and exploding barrel-carrying enemies. A chest in this vicinity contains four additional wedges.
3.  **Sir Alonne’s Throne Room (1 Wedge):** The final wedge is the hardest to obtain. It requires you to enter the Memory of the Old Iron King and defeat Sir Alonne, one of the most difficult bosses in the game.

## Detailed Ashen Idol Locations

Once you have your wedges, you must locate the idols. Many are guarded by powerful enemies like the Iron Warriors or Prowlers. Understanding the layout is part of the **idols of ash requisitos** for a successful run.

### Idols 1 through 6: The Upper Tower
The first few idols are relatively easy to find but dangerous to approach. The third idol, in particular, is located in a room with an Iron Warrior who is constantly healed by the idol's presence. It is often best to run past the enemies, insert the wedge, and then deal with the threats once their healing source is gone.

The fourth idol is located in a "Curse Tower." This area is notorious for rapid curse build-up. Equipping the Hollow Skin mask or the Cursebite Ring is highly recommended here to prevent frequent deaths and hollowings.

### Idols 7 through 10: The Fume Knight’s Sanctuary
As you descend to the lowest level of Brume Tower, you will reach the Lowermost Floor bonfire. This area serves as the gateway to the Fume Knight. Four Ashen Idols are placed in alcoves surrounding the boss fog gate.

Destroying these is a mandatory part of the **idols of ash requisitos** if you want a fair fight. If left active, these idols will cast a warm glow that heals the Fume Knight whenever he stands near the edge of the arena. 

| Idol Number | Location Detail | Danger Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Idol 7** | Left side of the Fume Knight fog gate | High (Boss proximity) |
| **Idol 8** | Right side of the Fume Knight fog gate | High (Boss proximity) |
| **Idol 9** | Far left alcove near the bonfire | Low |
| **Idol 10** | Far right alcove near the bonfire | Low |

## The Final Fragments: Fume Knight and Sir Alonne

Meeting the **idols of ash requisitos** culminates in two legendary boss battles. After you have used 10 wedges on the idols found throughout the tower, you will still be missing two fragments of Nadalia’s soul.

1.  **Fragment 11:** This is held by the Fume Knight (Raime). Upon his defeat, the soul fragment is automatically added to your inventory along with the Soul of the Fume Knight.
2.  **Fragment 12:** To get the final wedge for the final idol, you must travel into the Memory of the Old Iron King. This requires the Ashen Mist Heart from the base game. After defeating Sir Alonne, interact with the chair in the back of his boss room to receive the 11th Smelter Wedge.

With this final wedge, return to the Foyer bonfire. There is a side room nearby—often filled with enemies and curse mist—where the 11th and final Ashen Idol resides. Inserting the last wedge here will complete the set.

> **💡 Tip:** The fight against Sir Alonne is widely considered one of the fastest in the game. Using a high-stability shield or mastering the "roll-to-the-right" strategy is essential for survival in 2026.

## Rewards for Completing the Soul of Nadalia

Once all 12 fragments are in your possession, they will automatically combine into the **True Soul of Nadalia, Bride of Ash**. This soul can be traded with Straid of Olaphis for powerful rewards that are staples in high-level PvP and PvE builds.

*   **Outcry (Pyromancy):** Requires 45,000 souls. It is an upgraded version of Firestorm that casts faster and deals massive dark-fire damage in a radius around the caster.
*   **Chime of Screams:** Can be traded with Weaponsmith Ornifex. This chime has a unique casting speed mechanic and is favored by niche miracle and hex builds.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I complete the Soul of Nadalia without defeating Sir Alonne?**

A: No. One of the primary **idols of ash requisitos** is obtaining the 11th Smelter Wedge, which is only found in the throne room after defeating Sir Alonne. You cannot destroy the final idol without it.

**Q: What happens if I use all my Smelter Wedges before reaching the Fume Knight?**

A: If you used your initial wedges on idols in the upper tower, you may find yourself at the Fume Knight's arena with his healing idols still active. You must explore the "Barrel Hallway" to find the second stack of wedges to disable them.

**Q: Is the Ashen Mist Heart mandatory for the Idols of Ash?**

A: Yes. The Ashen Mist Heart is required to enter the Iron King's Memory. Since the final Smelter Wedge is located inside that memory, you cannot complete the Soul of Nadalia without progressing the main story of Dark Souls 2 up to the Ancient Dragon.

**Q: Does the Soul of Nadalia carry over to New Game Plus (NG+)?**

A: Yes, the soul fragments and the completed True Soul stay in your inventory. However, the Smelter Wedges are key items and will be removed from your inventory when you start a new journey, requiring you to collect them again.
