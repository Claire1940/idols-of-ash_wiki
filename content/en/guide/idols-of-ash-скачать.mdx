## Survival Strategies for the Depths

To survive the later stages of *Idols of Ash*, you must be aware of your surroundings beyond just the next grapple point. The environment is your greatest enemy, but it can also be your greatest ally.

1. **Listen for Audio Cues:** Enemies often make distinct skittering sounds before they appear on screen. Use spatial audio to determine which side of the cliff a threat is coming from.
