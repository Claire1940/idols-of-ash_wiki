## Understanding the Idol of Ash (Rebird)

The Idol of Ash, primarily known within the game as the Rebird dragon, is a cornerstone of the Ancient World's economy. Unlike standard dragons that produce Gold, the Rebird specializes in generating Platinum. This resource is used to craft crystals, upgrade habitats, and summon even more powerful ancient beings.

The Rebird is often referred to as a "Phoenix Dragon" by the community due to its fiery aesthetic and rebirth-themed animations. As you progress through the evolution levels, the dragon undergoes significant physical changes, growing in size and complexity. By the time it reaches its final stage, it becomes one of the largest entities in your habitat, literally overshadowing its peers.

> **💡 Tip:** Always prioritize evolving your highest-producing Platinum dragons first. The faster you generate Platinum, the quicker you can craft the high-level crystals needed for late-game summons.

## Maximizing Platinum Production

The ultimate goal of focusing on the **idols of ash ios** Rebird evolution is the massive boost in Platinum production. At Level 1, the dragon produces a negligible amount of currency. However, once you reach the Max Form (Level 5), the production jumps significantly.

*   **Base Production:** ~10-15 Platinum per hour.
*   **Max Production:** 75+ Platinum per hour.

By reaching 75 Platinum per hour, a single Rebird can fund the crafting of multiple low-level crystals every day. When paired with other ancient dragons like the Piercing or the Guard, your Ancient World economy will become self-sustaining. 

> **Warning:** Crafting crystals costs Platinum. If you spend all your Platinum on upgrades before you have enough production, you may find yourself "stuck" waiting hours for enough currency to craft basic materials.

## FAQ

**Q: Why is my Rebird dragon not producing Platinum?**

A: Ensure the dragon is placed in a compatible habitat within the Ancient World. Dragons in the "Normal" world (the floating islands) produce Gold, while only those in the Ancient World produce Platinum.

**Q: How do I get more Sapphires for the idols of ash ios evolution?**

A: Sapphires are crafted in the Magic Workshop using raw blue crystals. You can also occasionally find them as rewards from the daily chest in the Ancient World or by sending your dragons on missions.

**Q: Is it worth using Gems to skip the evolution timer?**

A: Generally, no. In 2026, Gems are better spent on unlocking Hatchery slots or buying limited-time Heroic dragons. Since Platinum production is passive, it is usually better to wait out the evolution timer.

**Q: What is the maximum level for the Idol of Ash?**

A: The Rebird (Idol of Ash) reaches its final visual and functional form at Level 5. At this stage, it is considered "Max Form" and cannot be evolved further, though its Platinum output remains a top-tier asset for your kingdom.
