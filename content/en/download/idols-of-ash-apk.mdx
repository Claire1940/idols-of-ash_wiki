## Understanding the Idols of Ash Mechanics

Before venturing into the soot-covered halls of Brume Tower, you must understand the relationship between Smelter Wedges and the Ashen Idols. These idols provide significant buffs to nearby enemies—including health regeneration for the infamous Fume Knight—and can inflict a deadly curse on players who linger too long.

To "disarm" an idol, you must have a Smelter Wedge in your inventory. Upon approaching the idol, you will be prompted to use the wedge, which destroys the statue and leaves behind a Fragment of the Soul of Nadalia. There are 12 fragments in total: 11 are found within the idols, and the final fragment is dropped by the Fume Knight himself.

| Item | Total Quantity | Primary Use |
|---|---|---|
| **Smelter Wedge** | 11 | Used to destroy Ashen Idols and collect soul fragments. |
| **Ashen Idol** | 11 | Cursed statues that buff enemies and hold fragments. |
| **Soul of Nadalia** | 1 (Complete) | Formed after collecting all 12 fragments; used for high-level trading. |

> **💡 Tip:** Never enter the Fume Knight's arena without at least four Smelter Wedges. If the idols surrounding his boss room are active, he will constantly regenerate health, making the fight nearly impossible.

## Smelter Wedge Locations

You cannot progress through your **idols of ash apk** checklist without first securing the wedges. These are distributed in specific batches throughout the DLC.

1.  **Entry Point:** You receive the first 6 Smelter Wedges immediately upon entering the Brume Tower area, located on a corpse before you cross the massive chain.
2.  **The Foyer:** 4 additional wedges are found in a chest behind a locked door in the Foyer area. This requires the Tower Key.
3.  **The Memory of the Old Iron King:** The 11th and final wedge is your reward for defeating Sir Alonne in the Iron King's Memory.

### The Fume Knight's Lair
The most critical idols are located at the very bottom of the tower, surrounding the fog gate of the DLC's primary boss.

*   **Idols 7, 8, 9, and 10:** These four idols are positioned in the corners of the Fume Knight’s arena entrance. 
*   **Strategy:** Hug the outer walls to disable these idols one by one. If you trigger the boss fight before disabling them, the Fume Knight will heal whenever he nears the edge of the arena.

## The Final Fragments and Sir Alonne

After clearing the 10 idols mentioned above, you will still be missing two fragments to complete the Soul of Nadalia. The 11th fragment is obtained by defeating the **Fume Knight**. He is widely considered one of the most difficult bosses in the series due to his aggressive second phase and high fire/dark damage.

To get the 12th fragment, you must enter the **Memory of the Old Iron King**. This requires the Ashen Mist Heart (obtained from the Ancient Dragon in the base game). 

1.  Travel to the room at the top of Brume Tower with the glowing red armor.
2.  Interact with the armor to enter the memory.
3.  Navigate the gauntlet of Alonne Knights.
4.  Defeat **Sir Alonne**.
5.  Proceed to the throne room behind the boss arena to collect the final Smelter Wedge.
6.  Return to the Foyer bonfire and locate the final Ashen Idol in the cursed room nearby to claim the 12th fragment.

## Survival Tips for Brume Tower

*   **Curse Resistance:** Many idols emit a curse aura. Wearing the **Hollow Skin** mask or the **Cursebite Ring** is highly recommended to prevent your health bar from shrinking during the disarming animation.
*   **Crowd Control:** The idols are often surrounded by enemies that play dead. Use a bow to pull enemies away from the idol before attempting to use a Smelter Wedge.
*   **Elevator Shortcuts:** Always look for the pressure plates that activate the shortcuts between the Foyer and the lower levels. This saves time if you fall victim to the Fume Knight.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I get more Smelter Wedges if I use them on the wrong idols?**

A: There are exactly 11 Smelter Wedges and 11 Ashen Idols in the game. You cannot "waste" them, but you might find yourself lacking wedges for the Fume Knight's room if you haven't defeated Sir Alonne yet. Always prioritize the idols in the boss arena.

**Q: Do I need an idols of ash apk to track my progress?**

A: While there is no official **idols of ash apk**, many players use community-created checklist apps or mobile-friendly wiki pages to track which of the 12 fragments they have collected. Keeping a manual tally is recommended since the fragments all look identical in your inventory.

**Q: What happens if I kill the Fume Knight before destroying the idols?**

A: It is possible to kill him while the idols are active, but he will heal for a portion of his health whenever he stands near the edge of the arena. This makes the fight significantly longer and harder.

**Q: Is the Soul of Nadalia required for any achievements?**

A: Yes, acquiring the "Outcry" pyromancy is a requirement for the "Master of Pyromancy" achievement/trophy, which necessitates completing the Soul of Nadalia.
