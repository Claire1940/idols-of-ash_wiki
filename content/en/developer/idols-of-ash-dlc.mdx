Navigating the treacherous heights of Brume Tower requires more than just combat prowess; it demands a keen eye for the environmental puzzles introduced in the **idols of ash dlc** content. As part of the *Crown of the Old Iron King* expansion, players are tasked with silencing the fragments of Nadalia, Bride of Ash. These fragments are housed within eerie, soot-covered statues known as Ashen Idols, which serve as both obstacles and treasure troves. Mastering the **idols of ash dlc** mechanics is essential for any player wishing to fully complete their pyromancy collection or obtain some of the most powerful catalysts in the game.

In this comprehensive guide, we will break down the locations of all 12 soul fragments, the necessary Smelter Wedges required to interact with them, and the legendary rewards that await those who can silence the Bride of Ash. Whether you are struggling with the Fume Knight's healing or looking for the elusive final wedge behind Sir Alonne, this walkthrough provides the tactical edge needed to conquer the tower in 2026.

## Navigating the Idols of Ash DLC: Essential Items

Before you can begin dismantling the Ashen Idols, you must secure specific key items scattered throughout the tower and the base game. Without these, your progress will be halted by locked doors or inactive mechanisms.

| Item Name | Purpose | Location |
|---|---|---|
| **Smelter Wedge** | Used to destroy Ashen Idols | Found in stacks of 6, 4, and 1 throughout the DLC |
| **Tower Key** | Unlocks various doors in Brume Tower | Found on a corpse in the lower levels of the tower |
| **Scorching Iron Scepter** | Activates the tower's elevators and machinery | Located in the center of the tower's furnace area |
| **Ashen Mist Heart** | Required to enter the Memory of the Old Iron King | Obtained from the **Ancient Dragon** in the base game |

> **💡 Tip:** Do not waste your Smelter Wedges on the first idols you see if you are struggling. Save at least four for the arena of the Fume Knight, as the idols there will actively heal the boss during your encounter.

## How to Obtain All 11 Smelter Wedges

To collect the full Soul of Nadalia, you must possess 11 Smelter Wedges to destroy the statues. The 12th fragment is held by a boss, meaning you don't need a wedge for the final piece.

1.  **The Starting Six:** Upon entering the **idols of ash dlc** area, before crossing the massive chain bridge to Brume Tower, check the corpse on the left. It holds your first six wedges.
2.  **The Secret Four:** These are located in a chest within a room filled with exploding barrels and Iron Warriors. You will typically reach this area after obtaining the Tower Key and descending into the lower sections of the foyer.
3.  **The Final Wedge:** This is the most difficult to obtain. It is located in the throne room of **Sir Alonne**, accessible only through the Memory of the Old Iron King. You must defeat him to claim it.

## All Ashen Idol Locations in Brume Tower

Destroying an Ashen Idol rewards you with a fragment of the Soul of Nadalia. Most of these idols are guarded by powerful enemies or environmental hazards like curse auras.

### The Upper Tiers (Idols 1-3)
The first few idols are relatively easy to find but often surrounded by Ashen Warriors. 
*   **Idol 1 & 2:** Found early in the DLC. Walk up to the statue with a Smelter Wedge equipped and interact with it. The statue will glow and crumble, leaving a fragment behind.
*   **Idol 3:** Located in the infamous "Iron Warrior Room." It is highly recommended to lure the enemies out one by one before attempting to use your wedge, as the animation leaves you vulnerable.

### The Cursed Towers (Idols 4-6)
*   **Idol 4:** Located in a separate tower accessible via a chain bridge from the Foyer bonfire. This area is heavily cursed.
*   **Idol 5:** Found near the top of Brume Tower. You must have the Scorching Iron Scepter active to use the elevators to reach this height. Beware of Prowler invaders in this area.
*   **Idol 6:** Hidden behind a glowing door near the Upper Level bonfire. An Iron Warrior guards this specific corridor.

### The Fume Knight's Perimeter (Idols 7-10)
These four idols are the most strategically important in the **idols of ash dlc**. They surround the fog gate leading to Raime, the Fume Knight. If left active, they will pulse with ash and heal the boss if he stands too close to the edges of the arena.

| Idol Number | Location Detail | Danger Level |
|---|---|---|
| **Idol 7** | Left side of the fog gate | High (Heals Boss) |
| **Idol 8** | Right side of the fog gate | High (Heals Boss) |
| **Idol 9** | Far left corner | Medium |
| **Idol 10** | Far right corner | Medium |

### The Final Statues (Idol 11)
The 11th idol is tucked away in a cursed room adjacent to the Foyer bonfire. Because of the intense curse buildup, wearing the **Hollow Skin** mask or the **Cursebite Ring** is mandatory for survival while you perform the destruction animation.

## Claiming the True Soul of Nadalia

Once you have destroyed all 11 idols and defeated the **Fume Knight** (who drops the 12th fragment automatically), the fragments will coalesce in your inventory. You will receive a notification that the "True Soul of Nadalia" has been acquired.

This soul is unique because it is not consumed like standard boss souls; instead, it represents the completion of the Bride of Ash's tragic story. However, you can still trade it for powerful items.

### Transmutation Rewards
Take the True Soul of Nadalia to Straid of Olaphis or Weaponsmith Ornifex to receive your reward.

| Reward | Type | Requirement | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Outcry** | Pyromancy | 45,000 Souls | An advanced version of Firestorm that casts faster and deals dark-fire damage. |
| **Chime of Screams** | Chime | Soul of Nadalia | A unique chime with high faith scaling, though it has a slower casting speed than others. |

> **Warning:** Most players prefer **Outcry** for its high utility in both PvP and PvE. The Chime of Screams is often considered a collector's item due to its slow casting animation.

## Exploring the Iron King's Memory

To finish the **idols of ash dlc** hunt, you must enter the memory of the Old Iron King. This is located at the very top of Brume Tower, inside a room with a suit of glowing red armor. You must have the **Ashen Mist Heart** from the base game's Ancient Dragon to enter.

Inside the memory, you will face an onslaught of Alonne Knights. This is widely considered one of the most difficult "gauntlets" in *Dark Souls 2*. At the end of this path lies **Sir Alonne**. Defeating him is the only way to access his throne room and claim the 11th Smelter Wedge, which is required to destroy the final idol back in the main tower.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I get more Smelter Wedges if I use them all?**

A: There are exactly 11 Smelter Wedges in the game, which matches the 11 Ashen Idols. You cannot "run out" permanently, but you must find them all to destroy every idol. The final wedge is always found after defeating Sir Alonne.

**Q: Do I need to destroy the idols to beat the Fume Knight?**

A: You do not *need* to, but it is highly recommended. If the idols near his arena are active, they will heal him for a significant portion of his health whenever he nears the edge of the room. Using four wedges here makes the fight much more manageable.

**Q: What happens if I miss a fragment of the Soul of Nadalia?**

A: If you miss a fragment, you will not be able to create the True Soul of Nadalia. You must check all 11 idol locations and ensure you have defeated the Fume Knight to complete the set.

**Q: Is the idols of ash dlc part of the Scholar of the First Sin edition?**

A: Yes, in the *Scholar of the First Sin* edition, the **idols of ash dlc** (Crown of the Old Iron King) is included by default, though you must still find the Heavy Iron Key in the Iron Keep to access the area.
