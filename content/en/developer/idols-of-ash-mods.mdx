### Survival Strategies for Nightmare Mode

1. **Keep Moving:** The centipede is programmed to follow your pathing. If you stay on one wall for too long, it will intercept you.
2. **Abuse Momentum:** Instead of climbing down slowly, use long-range grapple throws to "sling" yourself across the cavern.
3. **Ignore Non-Essential Heals:** In Nightmare mode, stopping for a latent ember can often give the monster enough time to catch up. Only heal if you are at critical health (one hit from death).
